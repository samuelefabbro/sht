Privacy Policy

This section provides information as to the policies used in the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data provided when you book with VQ Travel Services - Super Hungry Tour.

In order to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and be transparent with our guests or potential customers, we inform you that the data that we may collect from you:

  • Name and Surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City, address and country of origin
  • Date of birth
  • Food allergies or restrictions

All of the above data is collected so that we can register you for the experience that you have chosen with us. We collect this information in order to identify and contact you. Your name, surname, and e-mail address will also be used to respond to your requests or inform you of changes to your tour. We also use this data for statistical purposes (city, country of origin) and direct marketing (e-mail address) if you sign up for our newsletter.

Unsubscribing from mailing list

You can, at any time, unsubscribe from our mailing list by clicking on the “unsubscribe from this list” link in the end of our emails or by sending us an email, to hello@superhungrytour.com, asking to unsubscribe.

You may also ask us to modify, delete or access your data we store in our system at any time by sending us an email, to hello@superhungrytour.com.

We will store and use your personal data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations (such as the requirement to retain your data to comply with applicable law), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

Any allergies, medical conditions or medications that may affect your health or participation must be communicated in writing before the event.

Refunds and cancellation

Tour refunds are provided if notified 48h in advance from the tour departure date, and it is possible to request a new tour date. This change must be made no later than 7 days before the tour booked is scheduled to start by sending an email to hello@superhungrytour.com However, this challenge is only deemed when confirmation is received from VQ Travel Services - Super Hungry Tour.

It is the guest's responsibility to be at the agreed upon meeting point at the meeting time (contained in the confirmation email). If you are not at the meeting point at the agreed upon time, a refund will not be provided.

Cancellation

Public tours take place with a minimum of 2 guests, and VQ Travel Services - Super Hungry Tour has the right to cancel any tour with less than 2 reservations. Guests will be notified and offered another tour date. If guests prefer, a refund will be provided.
