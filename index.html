FAQs

1 - Are food and drinks included?

Yes, of course! There will be plenty to eat and drink. We'll make a minimum of 5 stops, with food and drinks available at each. If you don't drink alcohol, we'll find alternatives.

2 - Is there a min. and max. number of guests?

The tour will have a minimum of 2 guests and a maximum of 10. If the tour's full, send us an email. We'll see if we can make room for more.

3 - Is the payment transaction secure?

The payment system is handled by Stripe.com, and your card details won't be stored in any way.

4 - Is it possible to cancel the tour?

The tickets are paid in advance. You can cancel for a refund up to 48h. before the tour, but no later. Send us an email if you have any extenuating circumstances.

5 - What language will the tour be in?

English.

6 - Can I shop during the tour?

No. But wine bottles will be available for purchase at some of the stops.

7 - What if the weather's bad?

The tour will go on. Bring a raincoat, boots and umbrella. In case of extraordinary weather, we'll let you know in advance and issue a full refund.

8 - Where and when does the tour start and end?

The tour starts in San Giacometto Square, next to the Rialto Bridge at 5.00 pm. It ends in San Bartolomeo Square at 8.00 pm. You'll get the full details in your email confirmation.

9 - I have dietary restrictions, can I still join?

Send us an email and we'll see what we can do. The tour covers Venetian specialties, which are generally fish- and meat-based.

10 - What about gratuities to the guides?

Tips are appreciated but entirely at your discretion.
